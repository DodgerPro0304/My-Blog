Places Must Visit

\

Universal Studios

I had visited in March 2021

Universal Studios Florida is a theme park located in Orlando, Florida. Primarily themed to movies, television and other aspects of the entertainment industry, the park opened to the public on June 7, 1990. It is owned and operated by NBCUniversal and features numerous rides, attractions and live shows.

Lego Land

I wish to visit there some day.

Legoland is a chain of family theme parks focusing on the construction toy system Lego. They are not fully owned by The Lego Group itself; rather, they are owned and operated by the British theme park company Merlin Entertainments.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Exelent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it 2 years ago and I loved it. You should have a previous understanding of the story, or you will miss a bunch of the fun that hides within this movie. But before you watch Avengers Endgame you should watch, Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panthew, Age of Ulron, and Antman nad the Wasp. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel Movies.

Review On The Movies

The Lion King
Exelent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it 2 years ago and I loved it. It is a really amazing movie, ranging from the Characters, the animation, the back story and the current plot, and even the animation!Of course even thought it is a live action "remake" of the original cartoon version of the movie it is still amazing, and its great for all ages. There is so much fun and singing packed into this one movie that it makes it the perfect movie to watch with your family.
